Frequently Asked

Your questions, answered.

What exactly do I get?
Your own branded swipe deck. You upload your curated interiors, set the style categories, control what they see. They engage for minutes. You receive their aesthetic DNA — dominant styles, colour instincts, contradictions they couldn't articulate. The brief writes itself.
What if my clients won't use it?
They will. Swiping is easier than forms, faster than moodboards, more honest than interviews. If they won't swipe for ten minutes, they won't commit for six months. You've just saved yourself three proposals and a ghost.
What if my competitor is already using this?
They might be. The designers who adopt early build client data and workflow habits that compound. The gap between knowing and guessing widens every month. The question is whether you're ahead of the shift or behind it.
What if I cancel?

How quickly can I get started?
Your studio is set up within the hour. Upload your image library, add your branding, generate a client link. Send your first deck this afternoon. Use it in tomorrow's meeting. The 7-day trial is full access — no feature gates, no watermarks.
Why isn't everyone using this?
They will be. The question is whether you're one of the studios who got there first — or one who watched it happen.
